Gödel's Theorems: A Bird's Eye View

I wrote this article during Summer 2022, in an attempt to distill the most essential part of the argument of Gödel's famous proof. It is based on my reading and interpretation of Gödel's Proof (by Nagel and Newman) - which I very highly recommmend. The actual mechanics of Gödel Numbering are very cool, in my opinion (and not really that hard to understand), but to avoid burdening the reader with extra cognitive load, I consider Gödel Numbering to be basically a barcode system for arithmetical statements.

So far I only talk about the First Incompleteness Theorem (concerning the provability of statements). When I get the chance, I will put a follow up article here, about the Second Incompleteness Theorem (concerning consistency of formal systems).
